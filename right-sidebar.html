Multilingual Team

Our multilingual team offers professional support in English, Spanish, and French, serving both international teams and local organizations that require unilingual guidance to communicate effectively with their target audiences.

Through simultaneous interpretation, translation, and facilitation, we combine in-house expertise with a global network of capable language professionals. We have organized and managed interpretation during UN side events, as well as in high-profile venues such as Conference Room 4 in New York, and in Geneva and Nairobi.

Language is not only about words; it conveys culture, nuance, and connection. Providing accurate and context-sensitive language support is an integral part of our communications services, enabling organizations to maximize their impact at the UN and other international forums.
