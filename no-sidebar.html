Your Impact at the UN

To maximise your impact at the United Nations (UN) through strategic and targeted interventions, we help you take the next step, amplifying your work so it gains visibility and creates meaningful change for your community at the international level. Our support ranges from online advocacy actions to long-term program development, helping you identify key opportunities based on your work, vision, and the impact you aim to achieve. Your thematic focus, geographic context, and legal status shape the options available to you, enabling contributions through specific, targeted interventions that impact key stakeholders. By aligning your objectives with the right channels and audiences at the United Nations, we ensure your initiatives reach the decision-makers and stakeholders who can transform your ideas into real-world impact.

Online advocacy is the starting point for any engagement with the United Nations. Through video statements, virtual meetings, and participation in forums, you can intervene and make your voice heard. These activities allow your perspectives to be amplified, reaching decision-makers and global audiences while laying the foundation for broader advocacy and long-term impact.
